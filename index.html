ABOUT US

SUCCESSFUL JOURNAL PUBLICATIONS WITH ENHANCED ACADEMIC CREDIBILITY

MediDent (MDP) Research Professionals and Publishing services are an academic consultancy firm for PhD Scholars, researchers, academic staff (Professors, Assistant professors, Associate professors and lecturers) and research beginners. MDRP provides research planning, questionare designing, Editing, Proofreading, and Journal Publication services. Our team of experts ensures that your work gets recognition at the highest academic levels with publications in recognized international journals. Our successful publications have been accepted by ISI – Web of Science Journals in over 100 high impact journals indexed at the highest level (ISI-Thomson Reuters, Science Direct, Scopus, PubMed, Web of Science, Web Of knowledge, Medline). Our dedicated team of consultants and high end achieved experts from top North Americana and European Universities provides services in health sciences. Our clients have achieved highest academic ranks with publication in high Impact factor journals, Including Q1 (Quartile 1) original research Publications. With the experience in academic research, our team is well equipped and aware of the highest publication standards and requirement at universities. Our Team we ensure clients promotions through timely and high quality research publications for single authors and co-authorships in research groups. We are also aware of the requirements of individual universities in the middle east and other regions in the world for academic promotions requiring ISI-Web Of Science journals.
